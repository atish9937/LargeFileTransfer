About LargeFileTransfer.org - The Future of Large File Transfer

Why We Created LargeFileTransfer.org

LargeFileTransfer.org was born out of frustration with the existing file sharing landscape. Like many of you, we constantly faced the dreaded "file too large" error when trying to email important documents, videos, or project files. We watched as colleagues struggled to share work files across teams, freelancers waited hours for uploads to complete, and families gave up trying to share vacation photos because they exceeded arbitrary size limits.

But the real breaking point came when we realized the privacy implications of traditional file sharing services. Every time you upload a file to a cloud service, you're trusting that company with your data. Your files sit on their servers‚Äîsometimes for weeks‚Äîvulnerable to data breaches, unauthorized access, or even company policies that claim rights to scan your content. For sensitive business documents, confidential legal files, or personal data, this simply wasn't acceptable.

We asked ourselves: Why should sending a file be any different from having a private conversation? When you talk to someone in person, your words don't pass through a third party. So why should your files?

Our Mission: Privacy-First File Sharing for Everyone

LargeFileTransfer.org was built on three core principles:

  1. Privacy is a right, not a feature. Your files should never touch our servers or anyone else's. Period.
  2. File size shouldn't dictate how you work. Whether it's a 5MB document or a 10GB video project, you should be able to share it freely.
  3. Simplicity matters. No accounts, no subscriptions, no complicated setups. Just share and go.

We believe that secure file transfer shouldn't require a computer science degree or a monthly subscription. It should be as simple as sending a link, as fast as a direct connection allows, and as private as a face-to-face conversation.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional file sharing methods all have significant drawbacks:

  • Email: Limited to 20-25MB, which barely covers a few photos or a short video
  • Cloud storage services: Require uploads, storage management, and often paid subscriptions for large files
  • Traditional file transfer sites: Store your files on their servers, creating privacy risks and slow transfer speeds
  • USB drives: Require physical proximity and can be lost or stolen

Each of these solutions asks you to compromise on speed, privacy, convenience, or all three. We refused to accept that compromise.

Our Solution: True Peer-to-Peer Technology

LargeFileTransfer.org leverages cutting-edge WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) technology to establish direct, encrypted connections between your device and your recipient's device. This means:

  • Your files never touch our servers. They go directly from you to your recipient, encrypted end-to-end.
  • Transfer speeds are limited only by your internet connection‚Äînot by server upload/download queues.
  • No storage limits or file quotas. We don't store your files, so we don't need to limit them.
  • No accounts or tracking. We don't need to know who you are or what you're sharing.

The Technology Behind the Magic

LargeFileTransfer.org uses WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication), a powerful open-source technology supported by all modern browsers. WebRTC creates secure, encrypted peer-to-peer connections that allow data to flow directly between devices without intermediaries.

Our implementation includes:

  • DTLS-SRTP encryption for all data in transit
  • Automatic NAT traversal to work behind firewalls and routers
  • Adaptive bitrate streaming to optimize transfer speeds
  • Chunk-based transfers with progress tracking and resume capability
  • Browser File System Access API for large file streaming directly to disk
